My name is Diana Vera, and I'm a self-taught artist
based in Chicago, Illinois. I'm currently pursuing a
degree in software but art will forever be my passion.
Any time I'm not busy with school, I create custom
paintings and drawings for people who enjoy my work.
I use watercolor, acrylic, and oil paints on a variety
of surfaces, as well as ink and graphite drawings.

You can read more on theFAQpage.
If you're looking to request a piece OR
you're another artist looking to collaborate,
Hit me upon social media.
I'm always open to try new things,
so if you've got ideas I'd love to hear them!
