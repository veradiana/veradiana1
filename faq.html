  • Q. How can I request custom artwork?

    My contact info is on theContact Mepage. I use Snapchat and e-mail the most, so I'll likely respond more quickly that way.

  • Q. What are the options for requesting a custom piece?

    Pretty much whatever you want. I have all kinds of paint (oil, acrylic, watercolor, gouache) and whatever I don't have I can buy. If you have a specific canvas in mind (clothes / shoes / wood / etc) just let me know and I will purchase them. I can also do digital painting on my Wacom tablet.

  • Q. Can I buy pieces displayed in theGallery?

    That depends! Just ask me and I'll let you know. Some of the art displayed has already been sold, but any original drawings or prints are indeed for sale.

  • Q. How much do paintings cost?

    For custom pieces, the price depends on the materials and size of the work. That said, prints of existing pieces are $10 each if you wanna save some money.

  • Q. How long until I receive my custom order?

    I usually complete small-to-medium sized paintings in a couple days. If you live far away I will ship them to you.

  • Q. How can I pay for artwork?

    I have PayPal (@dianavera1996) and Venmo (@Diana-Vera-2). I also accept payment via Facebook (Diana Vera) and Snapchat (@dianaxdiana), or just plain ol' cash works fine too.
